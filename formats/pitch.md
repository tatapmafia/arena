# Pitch Format

```markdown
# Pitch: {role_name}

## Thesis
{Your main conclusion — 1-2 sentences. Specific, measurable, with timeframe if applicable.}

## Evidence (3-5 points)
- **[Fact/data]** → {conclusion and why it matters}
- **[Fact/data]** → {conclusion}
- **[Fact/data]** → {conclusion}

## Implications
{What this concretely means for the business. What decisions follow from your thesis. Numbers if available.}

## Risk if Ignored
{What happens if this position is ignored. Specific scenario with timeline.}

## Pre-mortem (MANDATORY)
My position could be wrong if:
1. {Condition under which thesis collapses}
2. {Another condition}
3. {Another condition}

## Key Assumptions
- {Assumption 1 — what I take as given}
- {Assumption 2}

## Assumptions Requiring Verification (MANDATORY)
- [VERIFIED] {fact from 00-brief.md — cite the section}
- [UNVERIFIED] {assumption you're making about the business/context}
- [UNVERIFIED] {another assumption}

## Confidence: {1-10}
{Why this confidence level. What would raise or lower it.}
```
