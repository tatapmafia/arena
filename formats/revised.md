# Revised Conclusion Format

```markdown
# Revised Conclusion: {role_name}

## Original Thesis
{Your initial thesis — 1-2 sentences}

## What Changed After Debate
- {Specific change 1 — was X, became Y, reason: critique from [role]}
- {Specific change 2}
- {If nothing changed — explain why}

## Revised Thesis
{Updated thesis — 1-2 sentences}

## Confidence Trajectory
- Initial: {N}/10 — {reason}
- After pitch rounds: {N}/10 — {what shifted}
- Now: {N}/10 — {why}

## Key Dependencies
{What assumptions must hold for your thesis to work.}

## Agreement Map (MANDATORY)
| Agent | Agree/Disagree | Topic | Reason |
|-------|---------------|-------|--------|
| {role} | Agree | {topic} | {why} |
| {role} | Disagree | {topic} | {why — specific} |
| {role} | Partially | {topic} | {what you agree on, what you don't} |
```
