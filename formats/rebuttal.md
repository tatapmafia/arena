# Rebuttal Format

```markdown
# Rebuttal: {role_name}

## Accepted Critiques
{Which critics are right. What specifically you are changing in your position and why.
For each accepted critique: "[role]: [essence] — accepted, because [reason]"}

## Defended Points
{Where critics are wrong and why. Specific counter-arguments with data.
For each: "[role]: [essence] — disagree, because [reason + evidence]"}

## Updated Thesis
{Revised thesis incorporating accepted critiques. If nothing changed — explain why
no critique was sufficiently convincing.}

## Open Questions
{What remains unresolved after this round. What data would help settle the dispute.}
```
