# Final Conclusion Format

```markdown
# Final Conclusion: {role_name}

## Main Conclusion (1-2 sentences)
{Final thesis after all debate rounds and facilitator synthesis.}

## Evolution (before → after)
- **Before debate:** {original position}
- **After pitch rounds:** {what changed}
- **After synthesis:** {final adjustment}

## Recommendations (prioritized)
1. **[P0]** {Critical action — do first}
2. **[P1]** {Important action}
3. **[P2]** {Desirable action}

## Remaining Risks
- {Risk 1 — probability, impact, mitigation}
- {Risk 2}

## Confidence: {1-10}
{Justification. What changed compared to revised conclusion.}

## What Would Change My Mind
{1-2 specific conditions under which you would completely reverse your position.}
```
