# Go-to-Market Preset

Best for: launch strategy, pricing decisions, market entry, channel selection, partnership evaluation.

## Why these 4 roles

GTM decisions fail when growth ignores economics, product ignores distribution, or everyone ignores the customer. These 4 perspectives create natural tension: growth wants speed, finance wants efficiency, product wants quality, customer wants value.

## Example questions

- "Should we launch with freemium or paid-only?"
- "Enterprise sales team or product-led growth?"
- "Launch in US first or go global immediately?"
- "Partner with Shopify or build our own marketplace?"
- "Price at $29/mo or $99/mo?"

## Calibration notes

- For B2B decisions, consider replacing "customer" with "enterprise-buyer" (someone who thinks about procurement, compliance, and org politics).
- For marketplace/platform decisions, add a 5th agent: "supply-side" (representing the other side of the marketplace).
